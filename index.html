Where birds of a feather, stick together.

Here at the Roost, our goal is to build community with bird watchers across the U.S. We are dedicated to connecting bird watchers together in order to enliven our experience in bird watching as a whole. Do you have some awesome pictures to share? post them in our photo page! We also sponsor watchers who are particularly savvy, interested? flock to our "sponsored watchers" and read about our pros. if you're new, you can check out our "get started" page to learn about gear and how to enjoy bird watching on your own.
